## 配置

`.env` 文件下设置

### Gmail

Gmail 很慢……

```php
MAIL_MAILER=smtp
MAIL_HOST=smtp.gmail.com
MAIL_PORT=465
MAIL_USERNAME=carveybunt@gmail.com
MAIL_PASSWORD=osvapfoksbdikgpx
MAIL_ENCRYPTION=SSL
MAIL_FROM_ADDRESS="carveybunt@qq.com"
MAIL_FROM_NAME="${APP_NAME}"
```
